Play Tetris

Controls

  • Click spacebar for immediate drop
  • Click P to reset or the Reset Button
  • Click A to move left and D to move right
  • Click R to rotate 90 degrees and E to rotate -90 degrees
  • Click C to hold a tetromino
    • The Tetromino that is held will be displayed on the right

Point System

  • 5 point - dropping a piece
  • 40 points - removing a row
  • 100 points - removing two rows
  • 300 points - removing three rows
  • 1200 points - removing four rows
